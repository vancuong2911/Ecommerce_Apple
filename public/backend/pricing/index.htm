Frequently asked questions

Here are some of the answers you might be looking for.

?

How do you define a developer?

A developer is someone who is working directly or indirectly on a project that includes AdminKit. This means front-end developers, back-end developers, testers and designers.

?

Can I use the static HTML, CSS and JS files?

Absolutely. You can bypass the Webpack build step completely and simply edit the static HTML, CSS and JS files included in the package.

?

Do I get free updates?

Yup! We update all of our themes with each Bootstrap update, plus are constantly adding new components and pages to the theme.

?

Is there any support included?

You have access to the Software's online support services via email for twelve (12) months from the purchase date.

?

Are the images included in the package?

All images in the package are downloaded from Unsplash and can be used for free. You can use them for commercial and noncommercial purposes.

?

Is there a money back guarantee?

AdminKit come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. In order to ask for a refund, please contact us at info@adminkit.io.

?

Does it work with Rails? Laravel? ASP.NET?

Yup! AdminKit has basic CSS/JS files you can include in your application. If you want to enable deeper customization, you can integrate it into your assets pipeline or build processes.

?

What payment options are available?

Transactions are handled by Gumroad.com, a secure and trusted broker in digital content. Gumroad accepts payments through all major credit cards as well as wallets such as Paypal.
